About Us

Company Background

Darl Real Estate & Construction Company was Incorporated in 2023. The Company was formed to develop well designed, competitively priced Real Estate in Nairobi.

Our Vision

To be a recognised leader in the Real Estate and Construction Field in Kenya and East Africa, while maintaining our authentic level of services founded on the basic core values of integrity and partnership.

Our Mission

We are on a mission to enhance the associations we partner with; while operating in an ethical and socially conscious manner.

Core Values

  • Integrity: Highest standard of ethical behaviour and transparency.
  • Partnership: Success depends on partnerships with all stakeholders.
  • Excellence: Commitment to professional excellence and quality services.
  • TeamWork: Combining expertise to deliver optimum solutions.
  • Innovation: Creativity and ingenuity for continued success.
  • Leadership: Spirit of leadership instilled in every employee.
